PARMA

Parma is a city in the northern Italian region of Emilia-Romagna. It's known for its rich cultural heritage, including significant contributions to music, art, and gastronomy. Parma is famous for its Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, Prosciutto di Parma (Parma ham), and other culinary delights.
