MILANO

Milan is a vibrant city in northern Italy, renowned for its fashion, design, and cultural significance. Home to iconic landmarks like the Duomo di Milano and Leonardo da Vinci's masterpiece, The Last Supper, Milan offers a blend of historical charm and modern sophistication. With its bustling streets, stylish boutiques, and world-class cuisine, Milan is a dynamic metropolis that captivates visitors with its energy and allure.
