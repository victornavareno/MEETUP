"A day at the beach"

Event Details:

Date: 17/06/2024
place: Rimini
Time: all day

Beach Competitions:

There are a series of friendly competitions on the sand, such as beach volleyball, beach soccer, frisbee, and relay games. Winning teams will win a prize.

Water Activities:

The event includes a surfing competition. Also there are brief lessons for beginners and areas for practice.

Rest Area:

We provide tents and umbrellas on the beach for people to relax between activities.

Live DJ:

A DJ with live music throughout the day with the best hits of the moment. The party does not stop!

Food and Beverage Area:

Food and beverage included with fresh and varied options, such as BBQs, salads, and non-alcoholic cocktails.

Alcoholic cocktails are not included and must be paid separate. They are forbidden under 18

Transport from your city will carry you to the exact place and bring you home at the end of the day.

You will have fun. See you at the beach!
