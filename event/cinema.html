"Movie Night: A Cinematic Journey Across Cultures"

Join us for an unforgettable cinematic experience tailored for Erasmus students! Immerse yourself in the magic of film as we journey through diverse cultures, languages, and stories together.

Event Details:

Date: 5/06/2024
place: Cine d'Azeglio, Strada Massimo D'Azeglio, 33, 43100 Parma PR
Time: 18:00

Film Selection:

Discover a curated selection of international films representing different countries and perspectives. From captivating dramas to hilarious comedies, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Subtitles will be provided for non-native speakers.

Cultural Exchange:

Engage in lively discussions and cultural exchange before and after the screening. Share your thoughts, insights, and reflections with fellow Erasmus students from around the world. Learn about different cinematic traditions and storytelling techniques.

Snacks and Refreshments:

Indulge in a variety of snacks and refreshments available during the event. From popcorn to international treats, we've got you covered for a cozy and enjoyable movie night experience.

Memorable Moments:

Create lasting memories and forge new friendships in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're a cinephile or simply looking to unwind, Erasmus Movie Night promises an evening of entertainment, laughter, and cultural enrichment.

Don't miss out on this unique opportunity to celebrate the power of cinema and connect with other students in a fun and meaningful way. See you at the movies!
